What we know so far about [ Muse ]?

Muse is a generative creature inhabiting music scores that visualizes music through dynamic motion and morphing appearance. It reacts to music notation and user interactions, to "guide" musicians visually through the compositions. Invisible to the ear but visible to the eye, Muse emerges in environments where notation is aliveâ€”improvised, generative, or interactively drawn. It floats within the staves, reacting to the elements of music as if they were part of its natural habitat.
